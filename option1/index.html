About Kaitlyn

Kaitlyn Myers is a rising singer-songwriter, influenced by motown, soul, and pop artists. Her lyrics often reflect her passion for mental health and social justice. Overall, she hopes one day her music will inspire others in the way that so many have inspired her. She is beyond grateful to share her message to all who will listen!
